Software Engineering Phases

Requirements to customer release and post-release learning for an AI powered software quality platform.
Lifecycle
AI automation map
Quality gates
Continuous feedback loop
1

Product Strategy and Intake

Collect customer asks, roadmap inputs, quality goals, constraints, and release scope.

AI: summarize, cluster, detect conflicts
2

Requirements Engineering

Capture PRD/SRS, acceptance criteria, traceability, ambiguity, and edge cases.

AI: extract, validate, trace
3

Feasibility and Risk Analysis

Assess technical feasibility, testability, schedule, hardware, and lab capacity risk.

AI: score risk, forecast capacity
4

Architecture and High-Level Design

Define interfaces, flows, quality attributes, observability, and automation hooks.

AI: draft options, find gaps
5

Detailed Design and Planning

Break down epics, stories, APIs, milestones, test points, and implementation tasks.

AI: plan, estimate, validate checklist
6

Development and Code Generation

Implement product code, scripts, configuration, mocks, drivers, and integrations.

AI: generate, refactor, document
7

Code Review and Static Gates

Check standards, security, maintainability, complexity, and change impact.

AI: review, classify, summarize
8

Test Strategy and Design

Create scenarios, coverage model, regression suite, negative and stress tests.

AI: generate tests, optimize coverage
9

Environment and Lab Provisioning

Reserve labs, provision OS, prepare DUTs, connect devices, and validate readiness.

AI + Zion: zero-touch lab setup
10

Build, Integration, and CI

Compile, package, publish artifacts, run smoke tests, and enforce gates.

AI: triage failures, enforce gates
11

Test Execution and Monitoring

Execute validation, monitor logs and telemetry, recover from known execution issues.

AI + Zion: execution orchestration
12

Defect Triage and RCA

Classify failures, deduplicate defects, assign owners, and rank likely causes.

AI: cluster, RCA, recommend owner
13

Quality Analytics and Release Readiness

Evaluate release risk, coverage, defect trends, pass rate, and criteria compliance.

AI + DSA: predictive go/no-go
14

Customer Release and Deployment

Package release, produce notes, validate artifacts, sign off, and hand over support.

AI: validate package, draft notes
15

Post Release Monitoring

Analyze customer issues, field telemetry, escapes, and lessons learned.

AI: cluster tickets, feed backlog
Continuous improvement loop

Post-release signals feed requirements, risk analysis, test strategy, and release scoring for the next product cycle.

15 -> 2 -> 8 -> 13
