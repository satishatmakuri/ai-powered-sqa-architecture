⚡ Why 12 months, not 3 years
With Gen-AI agents, MCP and Skills, writing the software is no longer the bottleneck — agents, dashboards, MCP connectors and RAG pipelines are built in days-to-weeks, so an MVP lands by ~Day 90. The remaining timeline is paced by the things AI cannot collapse: integrating physical labs & devices (LaaS, Robotics, NVMe/SATA/eMMC/UFS/uSD/PSSD beds), earning trust (AI gates run in shadow mode behind humans until they prove parity), accumulating validation evidence, and organizational adoption, security & compliance sign-off.
Build time → collapses Lab / device integration → fixed Trust & parallel-run → fixed Adoption & compliance → fixed
Delivery Roadmap — Monthly Swimlanes
Build lanes light up fast and overlap (AI-accelerated). The dashed Trust & Parallel-Run lane is the critical path to autonomous release gating: AI decisions shadow human gates from Month 5 until they demonstrably match, then go autonomous in Horizon 3.

Horizon Objectives & Outcomes
Horizon 1 — Foundation & First Agents
Months 1–4 · Build the spine + front-of-lifecycle agents · MVP by Day 90
Objective
Stand up the 4-layer backbone and ship working requirements → test-design agents fast, proving AI value without touching production gates.
Key Outcomes
  • Nexus orchestration + Prism UI shell + Vault RAG + Bridge CI/CD live (Mo 1–2)
  • IntelliSpec & Compass in pilot use; RTM auto-generated
  • TestGen auto-authoring + Envoy environment provisioning
  • MVP demoable at Day 90 on 1–2 pilot programs
Pacing Item
Connecting to existing lab APIs & CI; data onboarding into Vault.
AI Coverage
≈ 40% by Mo 4
Horizon 2 — Execution & Intelligence
Months 5–8 · Automate the core loop + start trust-building
Objective
Reach zero-touch execution and AI defect/quality intelligence; begin running AI gates in shadow mode beside humans.
Key Outcomes
  • TestBot zero-touch execution + self-healing scripts
  • Sentinel auto-classify / de-dupe / root-cause defects
  • Beacon predictive QA + auto-generated reports
  • Parallel-run begins: AI decisions logged vs human gates
Pacing Item
Physical lab / device-fixture integration (Robotics, multi-device); defect-model accuracy on real data.
AI Coverage
≈ 70% by Mo 8
Horizon 3 — Release, Ops & Scale
Months 9–12 · Autonomous gating, closed loop, multi-product
Objective
Promote AI gates to autonomous (after parity is proven), automate production monitoring, close the field→requirements loop, scale to all products.
Key Outcomes
  • Guardian autonomous release gating + compliance sign-off
  • Pulse production anomaly detection & hotfix triage
  • FlowEngine drives full closed-loop STLC end-to-end
  • Rollout across NVMe/SATA/eMMC/UFS/uSD/PSSD
Pacing Item
Trust parity sign-off; compliance/regulatory approval; org adoption across product lines.

≈ 84%+ by Mo 12
