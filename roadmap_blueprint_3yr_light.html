Horizon Objectives & Outcomes
Horizon 1 — Foundation
Year 1 · Q1–Q4 · Build the spine + earliest test-authoring wins
Objective
Stand up the 4-layer platform backbone and prove AI value on the front of the lifecycle (requirements → test design) without disrupting current QA operations.
Key Outcomes
  • Zion.Nexus orchestration + Zion.Prism UI shell + Zion.Vault RAG store live
  • Zion.Bridge integrated with existing CI/CD & lab tooling
  • Requirements Intelligence (IntelliSpec) + Test Planning (Compass) in production for 1–2 pilot programs
  • Auto test-case generation (TestGen) piloted; environment provisioning (Envoy) automated
Exit Criteria
RTM auto-generated for pilots; ≥1 product line authoring tests via AI; platform SLOs & RBAC signed off.
AI Coverage
≈ 35% by end of H1
Horizon 2 — Execution & Intelligence
Year 2 · Q5–Q8 · Automate the core execution & analysis loop
Objective
Achieve zero-touch test execution and AI-driven defect & quality intelligence across the heaviest, highest-ROI STLC phases.
Key Outcomes
  • Zion.TestBot orchestrates execution with self-healing scripts (target 100% exec automation)
  • Zion.Sentinel auto-classifies, de-dupes & root-causes defects
  • Zion.Beacon delivers predictive QA metrics & auto-generated reports
  • Test closure & lessons-learned KB (Vault) feeding the knowledge loop
Exit Criteria
Execution & reporting phases at 100% automation; defect triage cycle-time down ≥50%; predictive risk model in use.
AI Coverage
≈ 65% by end of H2
Horizon 3 — Release, Ops & Scale
Year 3 · Q9–Q12 · Close the loop & scale enterprise-wide
Objective
Automate release gating and production monitoring, close the field-to-requirements loop, and scale across all memory-product lines.
Key Outcomes
  • Zion.Guardian automates release gates, security & compliance sign-off
  • Zion.Pulse monitors production telemetry, detects anomalies, triages hotfixes
  • Zion.FlowEngine orchestrates the full closed-loop STLC end-to-end
  • Rollout across NVMe / SATA / eMMC / UFS / uSD / PSSD with governed ModelOps
Exit Criteria
Field anomalies auto-route to repro & requirements; multi-product live; platform AI coverage ≥84%.
AI Coverage
≈ 84%+ by end of H3
