Survey Overview
Purpose, scope, sub-teams covered, and lifecycle phases
Purpose Statement This survey provides Solution Product Engineering leadership with a structured, data-driven view of how AI technologies are currently used — or could be used — across all teams and software engineering lifecycle phases. Findings will directly inform the AI transformation roadmap, enablement investments, governance framework, and prioritized use-case pilots across Mobile, Automotive, Server, Client/Brand, and Operational Excellence teams. Your candid responses are critical to shaping a strategy grounded in real engineering experience.

AI Maturity Scoring Model
6-level classification system with composite scoring across 7 engineering dimensions
Level 0
No AI Awareness
Composite Score: 0–7
No tools used. No awareness or intent. Engineering work is entirely manual and traditional. No exposure to AI concepts in team discussions.
Level 1
Basic Awareness
Composite Score: 8–14
Aware of AI tools; occasional personal experiments. No team-level adoption. Individual engineers may have explored ChatGPT or similar tools informally.
Level 2
Experimentation
Composite Score: 15–22
Active experimentation by multiple team members. No formal pilots or structured use cases. AI usage is ad hoc and not tracked or measured.
Level 3
Pilot Adoption
Composite Score: 23–29
1+ defined AI use cases in structured pilot. Leadership is aware and supportive. Initial results are being measured and documented.
Level 4
Production Usage
Composite Score: 30–32
AI embedded in regular engineering workflows. Measurable outcomes in at least one lifecycle phase. Governance and tooling are in place.
Level 5
Scaled AI Engineering
Composite Score: 33–35
AI across multiple lifecycle phases. KPIs tracked. Agentic workflows in use. Center of excellence established. Continuous improvement cycle active.
