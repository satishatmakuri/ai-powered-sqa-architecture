# AI Adoption & Maturity Survey
## Solution Product Engineering — Storage Solutions Division

---

## Survey Theme
**"Engineering Intelligence: Mapping Our AI Journey Across Storage Solution Development"**

---

## Survey Purpose Statement

This survey is designed to provide Solution Product Engineering leadership with a structured, data-driven view of how AI technologies are currently being used — or could be used — across all teams and software engineering lifecycle phases. The findings will directly inform our AI transformation roadmap, enablement investments, governance framework, and prioritized use-case pilots across Mobile, Automotive, Server, Client/Brand, and cross-functional Operational Excellence teams.

Your candid responses are critical to shaping a strategy that is grounded in real engineering experience, not assumption.

- Risk concern heatmap by category

### 8. Enablement Gap Analysis

### 9. Strategic Commitment Index

### 10. Open Insight Cloud
- Word cloud from open-ended responses (Q15, Q19, Q24, Q35, Q36)
